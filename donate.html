Frequently Asked Questions

How does the checkout process work?

▼

Clicking the donation button prepares a secure Payoneer invoice portal in a new tab. You can complete the payment using your Payoneer balance, bank transfer, or card, with our official recipient credentials pre-filled automatically.

Is my payment information safe?

▼

Absolutely. The Harbor is a secure full-stack application that never captures or stores your financial details. All transactions are processed directly on Payoneer's banking-certified payment gateway.

Can I request a refund?

▼

Of course. If you entered a wrong amount or changed your mind, simply contact one of the support captains and we will issue a direct refund to your source account.

Do I get special perks or badges?

▼

To keep peer conversations completely equal, healthy, and authentic, we do not sell public badges or titles. Your contribution goes purely toward keeping this haven safe, secure, fast, and completely free of advertising for everyone.
